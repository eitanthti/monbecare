The Problem

Breastfeeding is the gold standard, yet 80–90% of mothers rely on indirect cues to gauge intake. 35–44% stop early due to perceived insufficiency.

Vulnerable infants: Preterm babies need precise intake for development.
Combination feeding: No clear split between breast milk and formula.
Night feeding: Hard to distinguish nutritive vs comfort sucking.
Clinical burden: 5–7 minutes wasted reconstructing history; 68% records incomplete.

Our Solution

MonBe is a gentle, smart, breast-mounted sticker that listens to sucking, swallowing, and breathing to deliver real-time feeding insights—without disrupting nursing.

MonBe Daily: Real-time intake and latch quality.
MonBe Nightie: Sleep-optimized classification of eating vs comfort.
MonBe Care: Provider reports and clinical integration.
MonBe To‑Go: Discrete cover with integrated patch support.

How It Works

5‑Sensor Fusion

Acoustic microphone, micro‑vibration (jaw), temperature (letdown), 4‑electrode bioimpedance, pressure (suction).

Clinical accuracy target: ±5% (final product)

User Flow

1) Apply patch (30s) 2) Feed naturally 3) Tap phone to sync (<3s) 4) View AI insights
