MonBe

"See your baby feed."

The world's first consumer-accessible, multi-sensor breast-mounted monitoring system that delivers clinical-grade feeding insights through an effortless, privacy-first platform.

$2.31B Breastfeeding Accessories Market (2024)
$4.20B Market Projection (2033, 6.1% CAGR)
85.7% US Mothers Start BF (CDC 2024)
27.9% Exclusive BF at 6 Months (WHO)

The Problem

80-90% of mothers estimate milk intake by baby behavior alone (Journal of Human Lactation, 2023), leading to frequent misjudgment and early breastfeeding cessation.

Primary cessation reason: 35-44% cite "insufficient milk supply" - often incorrect perceptions (International Breastfeeding Journal, 2021).

Healthcare data gaps: 68% of feeding records have missing/contradictory data (AAP, 2022). Only 2 companies offer genuine milk intake monitoring despite $2.31B accessories market.

Our Solution

MonBe is the world's first consumer breast-mounted multi-sensor patch that captures comprehensive feeding data and provides AI-powered insights through smartphone integration.

Unique differentiators:

  • Only true breast-mounted patch (vs. scales, nipple shields)
  • Post-session NFC data transfer (no RF during feeding)
  • Zero manual logging required
  • Complete ecosystem: Daily/Nightie/Clinic variants

Technology Validation

5-sensor fusion system validated by Northwestern University research (Nature Biomedical Engineering, May 2025):

  • Acoustic: Swallow detection (100Hz-8kHz)
  • Vibration: Jaw motion patterns (3-axis MEMS)
  • Temperature: Milk letdown thermal signatures (±0.1°C)
  • Bioimpedance: 4-electrode tissue impedance (10-100kHz)
  • Pressure: Suction patterns (0-10kPa range)

Clinical accuracy target: ±20-25% vs reference scales (Northwestern validation: 12 mothers, 17 weeks, NICU + home)

Business Model & Economics

Razor & Blade Model with Strong Unit Economics:

• Reusable Core: $50-100 (3-6 month lifespan)
• Daily Patches: $30-50/month (subscribe & save)
• Software Tiers: Freemium + premium analytics ($5-15/month)

Financial Projections:

• Customer LTV: $400-600 (6-12 month usage)
• Gross Margin: 60-70% (blended hardware + consumables)
• CAC Target: <$75 (digital marketing + word-of-mouth)
• Monthly Churn: <5% (high switching costs)
