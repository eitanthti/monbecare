MonBe

"See your baby feed."

A passive smart sticker that turns breastfeeding uncertainty into clarity - naturally and non-invasively.

TL;DR

Before we dive into the full story, here's a quick TL;DR answering the five questions investors ask most often:

1

Why now & what problem are you solving?

+

Breastfeeding is optimal for infant health, but 80–90% of mothers can only guess milk intake by baby behavior, leading 35–44% to stop early over "not enough milk" fears.

MonBe is the first breast-mounted patch providing objective feeding data — solving the #1 reason mothers quit breastfeeding prematurely.

Now is the right time: affordable sensors + AI + parents eager for digital solutions that eliminate feeding uncertainty.

Market

Breastfeeding accessories: $2.31B today - projected $4.20B by 2033. 120M births annually worldwide - about 90% of mothers in Western markets initiate breastfeeding.

Expanding into FemTech and BabyTech with the same multi-sensor platform, enabling future extensions into sleep, nutrition, and early development using the same core hardware.

Problem

Parents constantly wonder – "Is my baby eating enough?" Existing solutions are often intrusive, inaccurate, or disrupt bonding.

The younger generation of parents expects digital, data-driven tools that fit naturally into their tech-enabled lives and provide real, objective insights into their baby's wellbeing.

Solution

A passive breast-mounted multi-sensor sticker with NFC sync.

Detects sucking - swallowing - breathing - suction - temperature and bioimpedance signals from the mother's breast, translating them into clear AI insights. No camera - no wires - no radiation.

Technology

Sensors: microphone - vibration - pressure - temperature - bioimpedance.

Reusable smart core + disposable patch with smart AI-powered technology that interprets multi-sensor data to deliver real-time feeding insights.

Post-session NFC transfer. Provisional patent filed Aug 2025.

Traction

MonBe Inc. officially incorporated in the U.S. Full concept architecture complete. Provisional IP submitted Aug 2025. Mother validation in progress – interviews and survey underway. Hardware design in progress.

Our Mission Alignment

Breastfeeding is the most natural and precisely tailored nourishment for infants — a living, adaptive system rich in antibodies, hormones, and proteins that nurture the baby's growth, immunity, and emotional connection with the mother.

MonBe is committed to supporting this critical foundation of life by helping more families sustain breastfeeding with confidence. We aim to bring confidence and calm to every parent in the world through the insight and light that MonBe delivers — See your baby feed.
