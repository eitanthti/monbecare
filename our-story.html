"Is my baby eating enough?"

For generations, parents have lived with this uncertainty.
The only answers were indirect — counting wet diapers, weighing the baby every few days, or relying on guesswork. These methods leave parents anxious, second-guessing themselves, and sometimes giving up on breastfeeding sooner than they wanted.

Scroll to continue

At MonBe, we believe it shouldn't be this way.
We believe parents deserve clarity, calm, and confidence — without disrupting the natural bond of breastfeeding.

That's why we created MonBe, a gentle, smart sticker that listens to your baby's sucking, swallowing, and breathing, and gives you real-time insights into feeding — without changing how you nurse.
